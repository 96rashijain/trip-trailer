Advanced Packing Tips from Your Wife

  • Master the layer system: Base layer (moisture-wicking) + insulating layer (fleece) + shell (wind/waterproof) = perfect for 15-20°C temperature swings
  • Phone photography strategy: Bring portable tripod for landscape shots, keep phone warm in inner pockets (cold kills batteries), clear 50+ GB storage space
  • High elevation awareness: We'll experience significant ups and downs - stay hydrated (3-4L water daily), take breaks if dizzy, pack electrolytes
  • Weather flexibility mindset: Mountain weather changes in minutes - be ready to add/remove layers quickly
  • Jain dining success: Call restaurants 24 hours ahead, carry backup snacks for remote areas, use dietary cards to explain restrictions clearly
  • Cash strategy: Many mountain areas still use honor-system parking ($5-20), small vendors prefer cash over cards
  • Footwear is critical: Waterproof hiking shoes for trails, comfortable shoes for cities - your feet will thank you
  • Trust your planning wife: I've researched everything including backup plans for weather and dining!
